    Introduction

    The name HEEZHUN TECH is the product of merging the names of our founders — Soon Hui, Pei Yee, Mun Chun and Kian Hun. All the founders share but one common interest — Gaming, as long as gaming is hugely popular with children and young people and gaming is still one of the top activities enjoyed by 5-18 years old online, with many of them gaming via mobile device, PC or using their games console. HEEZHUN TECH , is mainly designed forr building a PC to let our buyer enjoy it by gaming, education, work and more.

    History

    The history of HEEZHUN TECH is neither complex nor a long one as it started with a university assignment that involved all of the founders of this business. Soon Hui, Pei Yee, Yong Zhe, Mun Chun and Kian Hun were students of the Data Science Department in TARUC, Tunku Abdul Rahman University College from the year 2022. Everyone put their best foot forwarrd and brainstormed to create an exemplary platform targeted at Malaysians with consuming interests in PC.
